About

POPS stands for Privately Owned Places. People may not realise that POPS are quietly replacing 'truly public places'. It is not necessarily good or bad. Responsible private owners can redevelop a previously run-down area into a lively place with no cost to the public. The purpose of building the online POPS platform is to allow you to share your POPS experiences and find out more information. You can ask questions such as 'Can I hold an event in this place?' or 'Does the place have step-free access?' to avoid disappointment. You can also find out the regulation of different POPS, or simply look for exciting events around you.
